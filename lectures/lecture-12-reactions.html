Micro-demo: mechanism fingerprints

Dissociative (D): rate independent of entering ligand; 5-coordinate intermediate.
Associative (A): rate depends on entering ligand; 7-coordinate transition state.
Interchange (I): no distinct intermediate; rate partially depends on entering ligand.

Interactive: rate law to mechanism

Rate = k[ML4X][Y]

Mechanism: associative or interchange (Ia).
