Micro-demo: CN to geometry

CN 4: d8 strong field -> square planar (Ni, Pd, Pt).
CN 4: main-group or d10 -> tetrahedral (Zn, Cd).
CN 6: octahedral dominates unless ligand field favors trigonal prismatic.
